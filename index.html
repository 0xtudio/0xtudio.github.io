About Us

In the year 2015, we eagerly embarked on our game development journey with a vision to create immersive experiences. However, life threw unexpected challenges our way, disrupting our progress and hindering the completion of our envisioned games. Despite the setbacks, we persevered through the various stuff life presented, and now, with renewed determination, we are back to fulfill our original mission.
